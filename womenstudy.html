About the Department

The Advanced Centre for Women's Studies is dedicated to promoting gender equality and empowering women through scholarly research, education, and advocacy. Our interdisciplinary approach covers a wide range of topics, including feminism, gender studies, women's rights, and intersectionality.

Our faculty members are renowned experts in their fields, conducting cutting-edge research and contributing to the advancement of women's studies. We offer a diverse range of courses and programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to become leaders and advocates for gender equality.
