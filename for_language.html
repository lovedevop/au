About the Department

In keeping with the road map for the future growth of Sandbox, Faculty of International Studies has been established to meet the aspirations of the Youth of the seven new departments have been planned to be established under the new faculty, the Department of Foreign Languages (DFL) is the first one to get off the mark

For the Academic Session 2015-16, the DFL introduced B.A. (Hons) & M.A. introduced in 2018-19, Foreign Languages (Chinese, French, German, Russian, Spanish) with 100 students intake in B.A. (20 students in each Language) & 50 students intake in M.A. (10 students in each Language). Subsequently, Ph. D programmes will also be offered.

The DFL is envisioned ''to emerge as 'Centre of Excellence in the Foreign Languages Studies'' with the following objectives: -

1. Build a sound foundation for the future growth

2. Create a conducive environment for value-based learning

3. Collaborate with other premier organizations to bring in best practices

4. Imbibe a culture of quality research and innovation

5. Leverage technology
